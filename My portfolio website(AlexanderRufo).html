Alexander Rufo

Hi everyone, my name's Alex. I am 22 years old, filipino man who is currently resides in San Jose, California. I am 2D/Manga artist. I am currently attending Ohlone community college to get my graphic design degree which is art degree that is essential to becoming 2D/manga artist. Becoming manga or comic artist has been my lifetime dream. I always wanted to become mangaka(as manga or comic artist).
